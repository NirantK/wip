Rubio's Character Attracts Millenial Voters in Ames under the category: POLITICS
 Iowa State University students flocked to Florida Sen. Marco Rubio's town hall meeting in Ames on Saturday, hoping to find a Republican presidential candidate they could support. Most either arrived as Rubio fans or emerged from the town hall supporting him.  And though many of his policy platforms resonated with the millennial voters, their main attraction to Rubio seemed to be his character.
